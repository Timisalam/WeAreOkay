Lime Tree are a four-piece alternative outfit from North Dublin known for their energetic stage presence and groovy melodies. They formed in mid 2021 and have spent the last year performing and selling out their headline shows in Dublin’s favourite grass-root venues including Whelan’s, The Workman’s Club and most recently, The Grand Social. In 2022, Jamie, Jake, Jamie and Evan released their debut single “Too Late” which surpassed 3000 streams in just under 2 weeks. “Jangly guitars and funky baselines dominate the emerging outfit’s three and a half minute introduction to the music world.” - Hot Press. They are back now to release their second single “Seasons”, which has received a very enthusiastic response from the crowd when played live, and has quickly become a fan favourite.
