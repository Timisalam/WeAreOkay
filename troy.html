Troy Martin


Meet Dublin-based artist TroyOkay, whose music draws on elements of punk, jazz and hip-hop through the lens of biting, colloquial vocals. Likened to transcendental and disparate artists along the lines of King Krule and Mac DeMarco, Troy's work has propelled him to the centre of the experimental indie scene at the heart of the band circuit in the capital. At just 20 years old, this Asian musician has already become a force to be reckoned with headline gigs in the Workmans, Whelans and Bellobar, and a series of high profile support slots around the city. TroyOkays live show has a reputation for pumping, intense sonic atmospheres and carefully crafted musicianship - a swelling, cohesive five piece onstage, their sets are as frantic as they are meticulously designed.
