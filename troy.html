Troy Martin


Dublin's music scene is witnessing the meteoric rise of an exceptional rock and indie artist who is breaking barriers and captivating audiences with his unique talent. At just 20 years old, this Asian musician has already become a force to be reckoned with. Inspired by the likes of King Krule, his music blends introspective lyrics with a raw and soulful sound. Having picked up the guitar at the tender age of 6, his dedication to his craft shines through in every note he plays. With his mesmerizing stage presence and heartfelt performances, this rising star from Dublin is making waves in the industry. As he continues to hone his skills and carve his own path, the world eagerly awaits the next chapter in the musical journey of this remarkable artist from Dublin.
