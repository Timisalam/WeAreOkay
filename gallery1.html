Ten Hail Marys are a three-piece alternative rock band from Dublin. With Adam Cullen (Vocals) having songs left over from a previously folded project, he decided to flesh these ideas out with Jake Murray (Drums) and soon recruited Dillon Doyle (Bass). These songs would become part of an ever-changing and refined setlist featuring singles that can be found on their Spotify. Just one headliner and two support slots later, Ten Hail Marys begin their first Summer together with a Forbidden Fruit set ahead of London, Limerick and Dublin shows with a new single in the pipeline.
