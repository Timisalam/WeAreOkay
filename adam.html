Adam Carroll


Meet the visionary founder behind a cutting-edge clothing brand that seamlessly merges the worlds of rock and indie music with fashion, hailing from the vibrant city of Dublin. With an unwavering passion for both music and style, this innovative entrepreneur has built a brand that celebrates the rebellious spirit and artistic expression found in the rock and indie scene. Drawing inspiration from iconic musicians and subcultures, their designs exude a perfect balance of edginess and sophistication. Each garment is carefully crafted to capture the essence of the music that fuels their creativity. With an innate understanding of fashion trends and an unwavering commitment to quality, this Dublin-based founder has established a cult following for their brand, transforming the way music lovers express themselves through fashion. As they continue to push boundaries and reshape the fashion landscape, this visionary founder's imprint on the Dublin scene is unmistakable, infusing rock and indie music with a rebellious sense of style.
